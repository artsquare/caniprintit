About ArtSquare

ArtSquare is a service that helps artists get their artwork from canvas to the Web by connecting them with digitizers in their area. We want artists to get their work out into the world, and we're here to make that process as easy as possible.

Why 'Can I Print It?'

Artists need high-quality images to assure high-quality prints. We've built this tool to help identify whether your existing images are high enough resolution to print.
