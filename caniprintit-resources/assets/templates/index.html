About

ArtSquare is a service that helps artists get their artwork from canvas to the web by connecting them to digitizers in their area. We want artists to get their work out into the world, and we're here to make that process as easy as possible.

Why 'Can I Print It?'

Artists need to understand that their artwork images must be high-quality in order to assure quality prints. We've built this tool to aid you with your existing images that may or may not be properly cropped, lighted, etc.
